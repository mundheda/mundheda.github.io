Vedant Mundheda

I'm a second year Master of Science in Robotics student in the Robotics Insitute at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh. I work with Professor Jeff Schneider on AI for Autonomous Systems. My research interests are in Reinforcement learning, Generalization, and Foundation Models.

At CMU I have been working on enhancing PPO for long-horizon planning in off-road autonomous driving using teacher-guided Reinforcement Learning.

Research

My research focuses on developing generalizable robotic systems that can adapt to diverse tasks and environments, with the goal of creating intelligent robots for real-world applications. I am passionate about leveraging large datasets to enhance the capabilities of embodied agents, aiming to bring us closer to the reality of efficient and versatile autonomy.
